This is a website exploration game created by Konami in collaboration with the Fourth Boundary to commemorate the release of the remake of "SILENT HILL 2".

There are hidden pages on this "Historical Society" website, seven of which contain the "accusations of a man who risked his life".
If you find a page with accusations, you had better turn back immediately.
If you go deeper, you may end up in some kind of incident.

This site is an unofficial guide and contains hints and explanations for hidden pages and planted riddles. Please use this site if you get stuck.

About "SILENT HILL 2"

"SILENT HILL 2" is a 2001 survival horror game published by Konami, the second installment in the "SILENT HILL" series.
Let's explore the rural American town "Silent Hill", solve riddles, defeat the monsters that attack you, and clear a path to unravel the story.
A remake will be released on October 8, 2024.

About "The Fourth Boundary"

"The Fourth Boundary" are a collective of creators who weave stories in the ambiguous realm between reality and the virtual.
Some of their best-known works include Project:;COLD, someone's series, and Kagamino special juvenile detention center.
